## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I signed in but I don't see a wallet — where is it?">
    Your wallet is created automatically when you sign in with Google. There's nothing to install. If you don't see a balance, you may not have test USDC yet — reach out via live chat and we'll help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My first trade is taking a while.">
    The very first trade on a brand-new market warms up an on-chain contract, which can take a few extra seconds. Later trades on that market are fast.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I pay gas?">
    No. Gas on Arc is sponsored by Puls. You only ever spend your trading stake. See [Wallets](/concepts/wallets).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is this real money?">
    Puls currently runs on **Arc Testnet**. Balances are test USDC and markets are for demonstration. See [Arc network](/concepts/arc).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is my win rate calculated?">
    Win rate is your mark-to-market PnL across markets, realized as markets resolve. It drives your leaderboard rank and your forecaster reputation. See [Prediction markets](/concepts/prediction-markets).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
