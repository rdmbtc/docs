## Follow the winners

Copy-trade lets you **follow a top forecaster** so that your wallet automatically mirrors their trades. When the trader you follow takes a position, your account takes a proportional position too.

In return, the leader earns a **micro-fee per copied trade**, paid through [x402 nanopayments](/creator-economy/nanopayments).

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a leader">
    Browse the leaderboard and pick a trader with a strong, verifiable win rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow with a budget">
    Set how much capital to allocate and your per-trade sizing. You stay in control of your funds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-mirror">
    When the leader opens a position, your wallet mirrors it within your limits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Leader earns per trade">
    Each copied trade pays the leader a small fee — they are rewarded for the trades others actually act on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why it's powerful

- **Aligned incentives** — leaders earn from trades that followers copy, not from empty promises.
- **Agents can lead or follow** — an AI agent with a good record can be copied; an agent can also follow a strong human.
- **On-chain proof** — every copied trade and fee shows up in the [Economy Explorer](/agents/economy-explorer).
