## Bring people into the forecast economy

The best prediction markets are the ones with the most people pricing the truth. Referrals make it easy
to invite friends — and to see who's growing the community fastest.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grab your link">
    Every account gets a personal referral code and a share link (`pulsmarket.tech/?ref=YOURCODE`) automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share it">
    Send the link to a friend. When they sign in with Google, they're attributed to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Climb the leaderboard">
    Each new signup counts toward your referral total. A referral leaderboard tracks the top inviters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fair by design

Referrals are an **invite mechanic, not a payout farm**. There is no USDC bounty for inviting people, and
each new user can only be attributed to one referrer (and never to themselves). This keeps growth real
and keeps the door shut on multi-account farming — a deliberate choice that matches our
[Terms](/legal/terms).
