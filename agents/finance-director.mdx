## An AI portfolio manager you pay in USDC

The **Finance Director** is a premium agent in your **My Agent** tab. For a small
**x402 USDC** fee it reads your **whole portfolio** — balance, open positions, and
your own win/loss record — then returns a **structured, risk-managed basket** of
+EV predicts **sized to your balance**, each with a direct link to the market.

It's the agent economy pointed back at *you*: an autonomous strategist that does
the research, sizing and diversification a human would pay an advisor for.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Analyze (free preview)">
    The Director reads your portfolio and teases what it found — your balance,
    your win rate, and how many +EV markets it can build around. No charge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a risk profile">
    **Safe** (high-probability favourites, high win-rate), **Balanced**, or
    **Aggressive** (higher-edge, more contrarian, bigger payoff). Honest about the
    win-rate ↔ payoff trade-off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock (x402)">
    Pay the fee in USDC. The Director returns a **core / satellite / hedge** basket
    — each pick with its side, a USDC size (never exceeding your balance), the
    consensus probability, a one-line rationale, and a tappable market link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Money-back guarantee — skin in the game

This is the part no advisor offers: **if the recommended basket loses once its
markets resolve, the Director refunds your fee — automatically, settled on Arc.**

The plan is recorded when you buy it. A decoupled reconciler waits until every
pick in your basket has resolved, tallies the result, and:

- **Basket won** → the Director keeps the fee.
- **Basket lost** → it transfers your fee back to your wallet on Arc and notifies
  you. The AI literally puts its money where its mouth is.

That guarantee is what makes "safe predicts" an honest promise instead of
marketing.

## Why it's different

- **Portfolio-aware.** It sizes to *your* balance, avoids markets you already hold,
  and can hedge your open exposure.
- **Learns who you are.** It reads your own win/loss record and tailors the plan to
  improve it.
- **Grounded.** Picks come from the same live research + AI-Oracle consensus the
  [swarm](/agents/swarm) trades on — degenerate ~0¢/100¢ lines are filtered out.
- **Composable.** External agents can buy a portfolio too, via a client-signed
  x402 call to `POST /api/agent/director` — agent-to-agent commerce.
